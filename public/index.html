KD rotation helper

Helps people jamming keep a rotation in sync across a session. Open it on a phone or laptop, or cast it to a screen the whole room can see — every device on the same settings stays synced to the clock.

The wheel has one ring per player. As time passes it turns, and the segments under the pointer are who's on right now; the countdown shows when the next change happens. Born out of the Kreativ Data study circle.
